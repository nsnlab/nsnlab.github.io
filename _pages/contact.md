## How to get here

The hospital is located on the South Bank, directly opposite the Houses of Parliament, with excellent public transport links.

**Underground**
- **Waterloo** (Bakerloo, Jubilee, Northern, Waterloo & City lines) — 5 min walk
- **Westminster** (Circle, District, Jubilee lines) — 10 min walk across Westminster Bridge

**Train**
- **London Waterloo** — 5 min walk

**Bus**
- Routes **C10, 77, 148, 507** stop at St Thomas' Hospital

**Cycling**
- Santander Cycles docking stations on Westminster Bridge Road and outside Waterloo Station

For full directions and a campus map, see the [KCL St Thomas' Campus page](https://www.kcl.ac.uk/visit/st-thomas-campus){:target="_blank"}.
