## Yishu Chen

Yishu Chen (陈一澍) is a PhD student starting at 10/2025 and joining the lab. She has a Bachelor degree of Applied Chemistry and a Master degree of Psychology.
During her masters, her main focus was using Temporal Interference Stimulation (TIS) on left hippocampus to enhance episodic memory performance, and trying to observe the mechanism under this process using fMRI. 
Her PhD project is using TIS to modulate spefici abnormal neural circuits in depression, and combing multi-model to explore TIS's potential effects in such psychiatry disorders.
