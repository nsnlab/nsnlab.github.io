## Current PhD Positions

We welcome applications from motivated students interested in joining the lab. Current funded PhD opportunities are listed below.

### Holographic ultrasound stimulation for precise neuromodulation of brain circuits

This project investigates the use of holographic ultrasound to precisely target and modulate specific brain circuits, opening new avenues for non-invasive neuroscience research and potential therapeutic applications.

## PhD Programmes

PhD positions are typically funded through specific PhD programmes. If you are interested in doing a PhD in the lab, please check the following opportunities first, as we cannot offer ad-hoc funding:

- [CDT in Smart Medical Imaging](https://www.imagingcdt.com/){:target="_blank"}
- [LIDO DTP](https://lido-dtp.ac.uk/){:target="_blank"}
- [EPSRC CDT in Advanced Engineering for Personalised Surgery & Intervention](https://www.surgerycdt.com/){:target="_blank"}
- [KCL MRC DTP](https://www.kcl.ac.uk/research/mrc-dtp){:target="_blank"}
- [King's – China Scholarship Council PhD programme](https://www.kcl.ac.uk/study/postgraduate-research/funding/kings-china-scholarship-council-phd-programme){:target="_blank"}

If you are aware of other funding opportunities that you would be eligible for and would like to pursue a PhD in the lab, please do reach out with that information.

## General Enquiries

We are always happy to hear from highly motivated students (undergraduate and postgraduate) and postdoctoral researchers interested in joining the lab. If you would like to get involved, please email [Dr Ines Violante](mailto:ines.violante@kcl.ac.uk) with a detailed CV and a short description of your research interests and expertise.

## Fellowship Opportunities

We would be happy to support applications for independent fellowships and would consider proposals from strong candidates for a range of schemes, including but not limited to:

- [Marie Skłodowska-Curie Postdoctoral Fellowships](https://marie-sklodowska-curie-actions.ec.europa.eu/actions/postdoctoral-fellowships){:target="_blank"} *(autumn deadline)*
- [British Academy Postdoctoral Fellowships](https://www.thebritishacademy.ac.uk/funding/postdoctoral-fellowships/){:target="_blank"} *(autumn deadline)*
- [Leverhulme Trust Early Career Fellowships](https://www.leverhulme.ac.uk/early-career-fellowships){:target="_blank"} *(spring deadline)*
- [Newton International Fellowships](https://royalsociety.org/grants/newton-international/){:target="_blank"} *(spring deadline; for candidates based overseas)*

It is important to get in touch well in advance of the advertised deadlines to discuss potential opportunities and agree on a research project. Please contact [Dr Ines Violante](mailto:ines.violante@kcl.ac.uk) attaching a brief description of your research interests and a CV highlighting your current achievements and expertise.
